## 🚀 Overview
Old home movies, low-resolution clips, and shaky handheld footage don't have to stay that way. VideoProc for Windows uses AI to enhance video, and its VideoProc Converter AI edition focuses on making footage clearer and smoother. VideoProc AI can upscale lower-resolution video toward higher resolution while restoring detail, steady shaky clips with stabilization, and interpolate frames to smooth motion or create slow-motion effects. The tools are designed to be approachable: pick a clip, choose an enhancement, and let VideoProc process it on your PC with hardware acceleration for reasonable speed. Whether you're reviving family archives or polishing new recordings, VideoProc gives everyday users powerful AI enhancement without a steep learning curve on Windows.
